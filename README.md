# Green Buildings Study

## Overview

This project explores sustainable building design principles with a focus on environmental impact, resource efficiency, and occupant well-being.


## Objectives

* Understand principles of green building design
* Evaluate sustainable materials and construction techniques
* Analyze environmental performance and efficiency


## Areas Covered

* Energy-efficient building systems
* Water conservation strategies
* Sustainable building materials
* Passive design techniques


## Key Work

* Conducted literature review on green building practices
* Analyzed lifecycle efficiency and environmental impact
* Evaluated indoor environmental quality and occupant comfort
* Studied material selection and sustainable construction approaches


## Key Insights

* Sustainable design significantly reduces environmental impact
* Passive strategies improve efficiency with minimal cost
* Material selection plays a critical role in performance
